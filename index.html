About Us

Service 1: Sales spot - each end-customer pays

Perfume Vending Solutions offers quick, convenient access to a variety of high-quality fragrances. Simply pay per spray to try a premium scent on the go, without buying a full bottle. You can find our machines in high-traffic areas like malls and airports for easy, affordable access to your favorite perfumes.

Service 2: Service spot - location pays, full service

Perfume Vending Solutions provides a full-service fragrance experience for businesses at no upfront cost. We install and maintain perfume vending machines at your location, offering a range of scents for your customers. You simply pay a service fee based on a tiered pricing model (e.g., 50 sprays for X euros), while we handle the rest—ensuring an added value for your customers with minimal effort.

Service 3: Service spot - location pays, machine only

Perfume Vending Solutions offers a "machine-only" service for businesses that want to manage their own perfume inventory. We provide and maintain the vending machine for a rental fee, and you handle utilities and stocking perfumes. In addition to offering fragrances to your customers, you can also generate extra income by selling ad space on the machine.

We bring you premium perfumes at the touch of a button. Our vending machines are designed to provide a quick, convenient, and luxurious experience.
