Services

Service 1: Sales spot - each end-customer pays

Perfume Vending Solutions offers quick, convenient access to a variety of high-quality fragrances. Simply pay per spray to try a premium scent on the go, without buying a full bottle. You can find our machines in high-traffic areas like malls and airports for easy, affordable access to your favorite perfumes.

We install and maintain perfume vending machines at your location, offering a range of scents for your customers. Service 3: Service spot - location pays, machine only

Perfume Vending Solutions offers a "machine-only" service for businesses that want to manage their own perfume inventory.
